About Me

HEY, you! I'm Cara! Welcome to my portfolio.

I am an amateur web developer hailing from Denver, CO, the Mile High City. I grew up in Syracuse, NY and got my B.S. in Neuroscience at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, MI. After spending some time working for a start-up in the medical device industry, I decided to take my skills to a completely different industry in Park City, UT where I instructed skiing. How did it go? Swimmingly, in that I am a snowboarder who learned how to ski on the job, leading me to take on more of a babysitter role than that of an instructor. After taking a year-long hiatus from the West back in Syracuse as a chemist in an environmental lab, I packed my car and landed in Denver after spending an arm and a leg on gas while meandering around the country.

My hobbies include coding, rock climbing, reading, listening to house music, and dropping things.
